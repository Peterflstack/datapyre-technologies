About Us

Who we are

Built at the Intersection of Technology and Growth

Datapyre Technologies is a Nairobi-based digital technology company working at the intersection of performance marketing, intelligent software, and enterprise infrastructure. We exist to help ambitious businesses across East Africa build competitive advantages through technology — not as a cost centre, but as a growth engine.

Founded with the conviction that African brands deserve the same quality of digital execution as any global company, Datapyre was built to close that gap. We combine deep technical capability with commercial thinking, delivering integrated solutions that span advertising, development, automation, analytics, and cloud infrastructure.

Our Philosophy

We believe great technology work is inseparable from great business strategy. Every campaign we run, every system we build, and every workflow we automate is designed with a specific commercial outcome in mind. We do not offer isolated services — we build connected stacks that compound in value over time.

The name Datapyre reflects this thinking. A stack is not a collection of parts; it is an architecture. Each layer — advertising, web, AI, data, infrastructure — is designed to reinforce every other layer, creating leverage that single-service providers cannot match.

Our People

Our team is made up of strategists, engineers, designers, and analysts who have worked across agency, startup, and enterprise environments. We are practitioners first — we build, run, and optimise the same kinds of systems we recommend to clients. That credibility is the foundation of every client relationship we maintain.

We are headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya, with the capability to serve clients across East and Central Africa. Our work ethic is rigorous, our timelines are honest, and our standards are uncompromising.

Case Studies

Results that matter

E-Commerce Brand: 3.8× ROAS Improvement in 60 Days

A Nairobi-based fashion retailer engaged Datapyre after struggling with inconsistent returns from their Meta advertising. We audited their full funnel — ads, landing pages, checkout flow, and attribution — and identified three critical failure points: broad audience targeting with no creative differentiation, a mobile checkout that lost 68% of sessions, and zero post-purchase retention activity.

We restructured their campaigns around creative testing frameworks, rebuilt their mobile product pages, and implemented an automated email and SMS retention sequence. Within 60 days, return on ad spend improved from 1.1× to 3.8×, average order value increased by 22%, and monthly repeat purchase rate doubled.

Financial Services Firm: Lead Qualification at Scale with AI

A financial services company needed to handle a large volume of inbound enquiries from their digital advertising without adding to their sales headcount. Datapyre designed and deployed an AI-powered lead qualification system that integrates with their existing CRM, engages inbound leads via WhatsApp within 90 seconds of form submission, qualifies prospects through a structured conversational flow, and routes high-intent leads directly to their sales team.

Result: Sales team contact time with unqualified leads was reduced by 71%, while the conversion rate from lead to booked consultation improved by 44%.

Hospitality Group: Integrated Digital Presence Rebuild

A regional hospitality group with properties across three East African countries needed a cohesive digital presence that reflected their brand quality and drove direct bookings — reducing their dependency on OTA commissions. Datapyre delivered a complete rebuild: new website architecture with property-level landing pages, a direct booking integration, a content strategy framework, and an ongoing paid search and social campaign to drive demand.

Within the first quarter post-launch, direct booking revenue increased by 31%, and organic search visibility for their primary location terms improved significantly following a structured SEO programme.

About Datapyre Technologies

Datapyre Technologies is a Nairobi-based digital technology company offering integrated services across performance advertising, web design and development, AI automation, data analytics, and ICT and cloud consultancy. The company works with growing brands across East Africa, providing the technical capabilities and strategic guidance typically associated with international agencies — delivered locally, with deep regional market understanding.

Datapyre was founded on the belief that African businesses should have access to world-class digital infrastructure and marketing capability, regardless of their size or sector. The company's name reflects its core philosophy: that technology works best when its layers are architectured to work together, each one amplifying the impact of the others.
